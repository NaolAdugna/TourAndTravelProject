EASTERN ROUTES -------

Harar

Harar is a historic walled city located in the eastern part of Ethiopia, in the Harari Region. It is known for its 82 mosques and more than 100 shrines, thus making it a hub for Islamic culture and architecture. Harar also offers exciting experiences like hyena feeding, trekking, and traditional coffee ceremonies.

Dire Dawa

- Dire Dawa is a vibrant city that is bustling with different cultures and traditions. Visitors can learn about the local culture and cuisine through traditional dance performances and food.

Sof Omar Caves

Located near Bale Mountains National Park, Sof Omar Caves are Africa's largest underground limestone cave system with unique rock formations created by water erosion. The cave system comprises of an extensive network of chambers, some up to 20 meters high and navigable for over a kilometer via narrow passageways.

Babile Elephant Sanctuary

The Babile Elephant Sanctuary is located in the Babille Mountains. This sanctuary houses a variety of animal species including elephants, baboons and baboons’ prey animals like lesser kudu, Soemmering’s gazelles among others.

Listing attractions or services as per-person pricing is a straightforward approach that allows visitors to know precisely how much they will spend per person for each activity or service they use. Prices may vary according to seasons or periods of the year. You may provide different rates for peak season and off-peak season based on the number of people and components included in the package. When customers require a unique experience or customized itinerary for their visit, consider an individual quote based on their specific requirements. Price methods of a tourism agency typically involve using dynamic pricing algorithms that take into account the demand for seats on flights or the occupancy rates of hotels.
