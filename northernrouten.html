NORTHERN ROUTES -------

Gondar Fasilides

Often called the "Camelot of Africa," Gondar is known for its well-preserved ancient castles, royal palaces and churches with unique architecture and design. It also has a vibrant cultural scene with traditional music and dance performances.

Lalibela

Known for its stunning rock-hewn churches, many of which date back to the 12th century, Lalibela is an UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the most popular destinations in Northern Ethiopia.

Simien Mountains National Park

This national park is home to some of Africa's most dramatic mountain landscapes, with towering peaks, deep valleys, and stunning views. It's also a great place for hiking and wildlife viewing, with rare animals such as the Gelada Baboon and Ethiopian Wolf.

Axum

This ancient city was once the seat of the Axumite Kingdom, and is famous for its uniquely shaped Obelisks, ancient tombs and ruins, Christian churches, and other architectural wonders.
