ADDIS ABABA AND SURRONDING ROUTES -------

Addis Ababa Museum

Addis Ababa Museum, situated in the heart of the city, is a fascinating destination for anyone interested in exploring the rich history and culture of Ethiopia's diverse ethnic groups. The museum houses a variety of exhibits, including traditional artifacts and artworks that showcase the country's ancient history and contemporary art.

Holy Trinity Cathedral Church

Holy Trinity Cathedral Church is an impressive architectural landmark located in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. The church which was built in the mid-20th century mixes neo-baroque and modernist styles to produce an awe-inspiring structure that draws visitors from all over the world.

Entoto Park

Entoto Park is a magnificent urban park located in the Ethiopian capital of Addis Ababa. Spread over 1,300 hectares of lush green hills, Entoto Park provides plenty of space for visitors to take in the stunning views of the city below. The park is a natural haven in the heart of the bustling city, with a variety of hiking trails through dense forests and open meadows.

Kuriftu Water Park

Kuriftu Water Park is one of the top destinations for water activities in Ethiopia. Located in Bishoftu, a town only one hour's drive from Addis Ababa, Kuriftu Water Park boasts a range of thrilling and exciting water-based activities and attractions. The water park features multiple swimming pools, both indoor and outdoor, with different depths to cater to swimmers of all levels.
