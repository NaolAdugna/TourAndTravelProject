SOUTHERN ROUTES -------

Bale Mountains National Park

Located in the Oromia Region, Bale Mountains National Park boasts vibrant flora and fauna, including rare and unique species like the Ethiopian wolf and the Bale monkey. Visitors can explore the park on guided hikes or on a multi-day trek to the Sanetti Plateau, where they can see hundreds of wildflowers and enjoy stunning views.

. Arba Minch

Arba Minch is a charming town located between two Rift Valley lakes: Lake Abaya and Lake Chamo. The town offers an array of attractions, including boat safari tours that provide visitors with an opportunity to observe hippos and crocodiles in their natural habitat.

Omo Valley

Located in the SNNP Region, the Omo Valley is home to several indigenous tribes that have preserved their ancestral cultures and traditions for generations. Visitors can experience diverse tribes such as Mursi, Hamer, Karo, and Dasenech, among others.

Hawassa

Hawassa is another stunning town located on the shores of Lake Awassa; visitors can have a picnic or go fishing while exploring the beautiful scenery around Lake Awassa.

Listing attractions or services as per-person pricing is a straightforward approach that allows visitors to know precisely how much they will spend per person for each activity or service they use. Prices may vary according to seasons or periods of the year. You may provide different rates for peak season and off-peak season based on the number of people and components included in the package. When customers require a unique experience or customized itinerary for their visit, consider an individual quote based on their specific requirements. When pricing tourism experiences, it's best to be transparent and upfront about costs so visitors know what to expect when planning their itineraries. By clearly outlining pricing options, you can help visitors make informed decisions about the experiences and services that fit within their budget. Price methods of a tourism agency typically involve using dynamic pricing algorithms that take into account the demand for seats on flights or the occupancy rates of hotels.
